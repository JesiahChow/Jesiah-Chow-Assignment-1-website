ONE OK ROCK is a Japanese rock band, formed in Tokyo,Japan, in 2005 and debuted in 2007.
Their members consists of vocalist Takehiro Moriuchi, guitarist and bandleader Toru Yamashita,
bassist Ryota Kohoma and drummer Tomoya Kanki.
